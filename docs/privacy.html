Privacy Policy for tap-tap-boom

Last updated: February 28, 2026

At tap-tap-boom, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

tap-tap-boom is designed to be a private, local-first interactive instrument. We do not collect, or share any personal information including but not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Your location or device identifiers.
  • Your usage patterns or musical creations.

All interactions and audio synthesis happen locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs or analytics services that collect personal data. We do not display advertisements.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions only for the purpose of the app's functionality:

  • Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback when you interact with the musical grid.

These permissions are used locally and no data associated with them is transmitted externally.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer contact provided in the Play Store.
